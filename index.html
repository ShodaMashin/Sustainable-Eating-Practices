SUSTAINABLE EATING BARRIERS

Why do we need to eat sustainably?

Climate change is a common topic of conversation and we hear the need to alter our ways in order to improve (and save!) our planet ... but what exactly can we do? Included in this website are three modules: The impact of our Western diets, sustainable food choices, and alternative proteins. It will take you about 15 to 20 minutes to go through these activities, and by the end you'll know the impact of your current diet choices and the potential you hold to make improved, sustainable, nutritious, and delicious food choices! Our friends Jordy, Clive, Brianna, and Alexis will also be learning about the impact of their diets as you do. Do you make similar food choices to them? Is there something you can learn from them? Let's find out!
